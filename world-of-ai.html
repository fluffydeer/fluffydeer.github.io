World of AI

This master thesis project is an educational game about artificial intelligence in virtual reality to be finished in June 2022. Game will consist of two levels and will be a part of a big project called Svet kremíka - World of Silicone. This set of educational virtual reality games will be then accessible for students and will be used to promote the studies in technical areas to high school students in Slovakia.

First level will take the player into a dessert where he will learn about decision trees. The tree was modelled in Blender.

Second level will show the player the principles of machine learning.
